Rockwell Automation Inc.

Rockwell Automation, Inc. is an American provider of industrial automation and information technology.
Brands include Allen-Bradley and Factory Talk software. Headquartered in Milwaukee, Wisconsin,
Rockwell Automation employs over 23,000 people and has customers in more than 100 countries worldwide.
